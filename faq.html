Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book appointments through our online portal, mobile app, or by calling our reception at (800) 555-1234. Online booking is available 24/7.

For urgent appointments, please call our office directly.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans including:

  • Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • Aetna
  • UnitedHealthcare
  • Cigna
  • Medicare

Please contact our billing department to verify your specific plan.

How do I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records through our patient portal. To sign up:

  1. Visit our website and click "Patient Portal"
  2. Click "Register New Account"
  3. Enter your personal information and verification code (provided during your last visit)
  4. Create your login credentials

For immediate assistance, call medical records at (800) 555-5678.

What are your COVID-19 safety measures?

We maintain strict safety protocols:

  • Mandatory masks for all patients and staff
  • Enhanced cleaning between patients
  • Social distancing in waiting areas
  • HEPA air filtration systems
  • Virtual visit options available

Please reschedule if you have any COVID-19 symptoms.

How do I request a prescription refill?

Prescription refills can be requested:

  • Through the patient portal (fastest method)
  • By calling our prescription line at (800) 555-7890
  • Through your pharmacy (they will contact us)

Please allow 48 hours for processing. Controlled substances require office visits.

Still need help?

If you didn't find the answer to your question, our support team is available to assist you.
